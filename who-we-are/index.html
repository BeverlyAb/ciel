CIEL

Project members

CIEL is currently developed by six researchers in the Networks and Operating Systems group of the University of Cambridge Computer Laboratory.

All queries should be directed to the project leader, Derek Murray.

  • Derek Murray is a final-year PhD student in the Computer Laboratory, where he leads the CIEL project. Derek is currently carrying out research into expressive programming models for data-intensive computing. At various times, his research interests have included code optimisation, high-performance computing, and OS virtualisation. You can find him on Twitter (@mrry) and Github, or send him an email (Derek.Murray@cl.cam.ac.uk).
  • Malte Schwarzkopf is a second-year PhD student in the Computer Laboratory. You can find him on Twitter (@ms705) and Github.
  • Chris Smowton is a second-year PhD student in the Computer Laboratory. You can find him on Twitter (@chrissmowton) and Github.
  • Steven Smith is a second-year PhD student in the Computer Laboratory.
  • Anil Madhavapeddy is a Horizon Research Fellow at the University of Cambridge. You can find him on Twitter (@avsm) and Github.
  • Steve Hand is a Reader in Computer Systems in the Computer Laboratory.
